Returns & Exchanges

Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase in original condition with tags attached. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging. Sale items and personalized items are final sale.
Use the prepaid return label included in your package or initiate a return through your online account. Package items securely and attach the return label. Drop off at any authorized shipping location.

Care & Sizing

Care instructions are provided on the label of each item. Generally, we recommend dry cleaning for delicate items, cold washing for denim, and following specific fabric care instructions to maintain quality.
Our sizing varies by collection and style. Some pieces are designed for an oversized fit while others are more fitted. Always check the specific size guide for each product and consider the intended fit.
Yes, but we recommend washing denim items inside out in cold water on a gentle cycle. Air dry to prevent shrinking and fading. Avoid bleach and fabric softener to maintain the quality and color.
If you receive a damaged item, contact us immediately with photos. For items damaged after purchase, our customer service team can recommend repair services or care tips to restore your item.
